Nagpal Lab

We develop statistical genetics and integrative genomics approaches to assess human disease risk, with the goal of advancing precision genomic medicine in India.

Koita Centre for Digital Health · Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

Research Focus

Polygenic Risk & Environment

Understanding how polygenic risk score–by–environment interactions shape the risk and prevalence of common non-communicable diseases.

Integrative Genomics

Integrating gene expression profiles with genetics to improve prediction of disease risk progression, particularly in inflammatory bowel disease.

Monogenic vs Polygenic Risk

Exploring how monogenic and polygenic risk interact — and to what extent polygenic background modulates the risk of rare pathogenic variants.

Ancestry & Health Equity

Developing ancestry-informed risk assessment tools to ensure precision medicine benefits extend equitably across diverse populations.
