Nagpal Lab

We develop statistical genetics and integrative genomics approaches to assess human disease risk, with the goal of advancing precision genomic medicine.

Koita Centre for Digital Health · Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

Research Focus

Polygenic Risk & Environment

Understanding how polygenic risk interacts with the environment to influence disease risk, and the clinical utility of PRSxE-based interventions.

Integrative Genomics

Integrating transcriptomics with genetics to improve risk stratification and patient outcomes, including disease progression, recurrence and treatment response.

Monogenic vs Polygenic Risk

Exploring how monogenic and polygenic risk interact - and to what extent polygenic background modulates the risk of rare pathogenic variants.

Ancestry & Health Equity

Developing ancestry-informed genetic risk assessment approaches to ensure precision medicine benefits extend equitably across diverse populations.
